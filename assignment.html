About Us

Dapur Mama Megumi is where the warmth of home-cooked meals meets the convenience of professional catering. Founded with love and a passion for bringing families and communities together through food, we pride ourselves on delivering authentic, flavorful, and wholesome Indonesian-inspired dishes right to your table.

Our Story

The journey of Dapur Mama Megumi began in the heart of our founder, a culinary enthusiast with roots in Indonesia. With cherished family recipes and a dream of sharing her love for cooking with others, turning her kitchen into a haven for food lovers.
